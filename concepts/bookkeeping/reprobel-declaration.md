# Reprobel declaration (reprography levy)

**Reprobel** collects a legally-mandated levy for copying and reusing copyrighted texts and images. Every Belgian business (sole trader or company) must file an **annual declaration** , a genuine obligation, not a scam letter.

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Two declaration types:** the **reprography declaration** (paper copies; a **nil declaration** is possible if you make none) and the **Bizili licence** (paper + digital reuse; no nil option). [search]
- **Cost:** in most cases at least **~EUR 35/year** (2026), unless you file a **nil** reprography declaration. [search]
- **Who:** every Belgian business that could copy/reuse protected material; you file when Reprobel requests it (by letter or email). [search]
- **Late / no filing:** penalties or an estimated assessment apply. [search]

> The amounts and the procedure change. **Confirm at [reprobel.be](https://www.reprobel.be)** before filing or paying.

## Why it matters

A small but mandatory annual admin item a one-person BV easily mistakes for spam. The agent should track the Reprobel deadline, file (nil if no copying), and book the levy as a deductible cost ([[expense-categorization]]).
