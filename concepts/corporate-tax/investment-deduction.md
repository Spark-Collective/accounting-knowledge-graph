# Investment deduction (investeringsaftrek)

A tax incentive: deduct a **percentage of qualifying investments** directly from taxable profit (on top of normal depreciation), lowering corporate tax. From **1 January 2025** the old fragmented system was **reformed into a simpler one with new categories**.

## What qualifies (verify_live)

- **New** tangible or intangible fixed assets, **used in Belgium** for the professional activity, with a depreciation period of **at least 3 years**. [astro]
- **Excluded:** assets not used 100% professionally; **passenger / dual-use cars** ([[car-and-mobility]]); polluting investments (unless no economically viable carbon-free alternative exists). [astro]

## The 2025 categories

The reform groups the deduction into a few tiers , a **basic** deduction, a higher **thematic/green** deduction (energy, transport, environment, digital), and a **technology** deduction , each with its own rate. **The exact percentages and category definitions must be confirmed live.** [astro]

> **verify_live.** Confirm the current rates and category conditions with [FOD Financiën](https://financien.belgium.be) before applying to a real assessment.

## Why it matters

For a one-person BV making a real investment (equipment, green assets), this can materially cut the year's [[corporate-tax-basics|corporate tax]] , the agent should check eligibility when booking a fixed asset ([[chart-of-accounts-mar]]).
