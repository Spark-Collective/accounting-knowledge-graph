# EV home-charging reimbursement

If the director charges an electric [[car-and-mobility|company car]] at home, the company can reimburse the electricity. There are **two methods**, and the choice affects both administration and amount.

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Actual cost per kWh:** precise reimbursement of the real electricity cost; needs more proof (a calibrated charger/meter and the actual tariff), but can be better when the home electricity price is above the forfait. [astro]
- **Forfait per kWh:** a fixed rate per kWh, simpler, but it depends on **regional tariffs** and is **updated periodically** (the 2025 forfait varies by region/quarter). [astro]

> The forfait rate changes (regionally, roughly quarterly). **Confirm the current per-kWh forfait** before reimbursing; an over-reimbursement becomes a taxable benefit.

## Why it matters

A practical, recurring calculation for an EV-driving one-person BV. The reimbursement sits alongside the car's [[benefits-in-kind-vaa|VAA]] and its [[car-and-mobility|CO2-based deductibility]] , the agent picks the method and applies the current forfait.
