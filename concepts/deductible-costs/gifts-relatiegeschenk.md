# Relationship gifts (relatiegeschenken)

Business gifts to clients (gift vouchers, bottles, etc.) are partly deductible, with a separate VAT rule. A flexible option is a **gift voucher** (boekenbon, cinema ticket, an online-shop voucher), usable all year, not only at the holidays.

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Income tax: ~50% deductible** as a representation cost. [astro]
- **VAT: deductible for only one gift per client per calendar year**, and only if the gift is **under ~EUR 50 (excl. VAT)**. Two gifts to the same client -> VAT on only one; ten gifts to ten different clients -> all fine. [astro]
- **Document the recipient's name** on the purchase document when booking, for a control. [astro]

> Confirm the current EUR 50 cap and the 50% rate with [FOD Financiën](https://financien.belgium.be).

## Booking

Book to a class-6 representation account; the non-deductible 50% is a year-end add-back ([[expense-categorization]] flags it). Apply the [[vat-deduction-conditions|VAT-deduction]] one-gift-per-client cap. The 50% logic mirrors [[restaurant-and-meals|representation costs]] and the benefits side of [[sponsoring]].
