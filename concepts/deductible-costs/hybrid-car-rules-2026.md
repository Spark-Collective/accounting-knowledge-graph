# Hybrid car deductibility from 2026

Part of the ongoing fossil-vehicle phase-out (see [[car-and-mobility]]). The rules below are for **corporate tax** (personal income tax keeps a separate transition regime).

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Hybrid cars ordered from 1 January 2026 are no longer deductible** in the company. [astro]
- **Order date drives the regime**, not the delivery or payment date. [astro]
- **Grandfathering:** a car **ordered before 1 July 2023** keeps the deductibility that applied at the order moment for its whole life (while it stays with the same owner). [astro]
- The treatment is evolving (an earlier transition regime was changed to align with EU law), so this is a fast-moving area. [astro]

> This is a **fast-changing** rule. **Always confirm with [FOD Financiën](https://financien.belgium.be) or the accountant before ordering, leasing, or renting a car.**

## Why it matters

The order date is decisive and the window is closing , a concrete timing decision the agent should flag before any vehicle commitment. Interacts with the CO2-based deductibility and VAA in [[car-and-mobility]] and [[benefits-in-kind-vaa]].
