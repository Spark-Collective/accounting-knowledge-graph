# ICT equipment (iPad, tablet, laptop)

Bought through the company, an iPad/tablet (and similar ICT) is fully deductible for income tax, with a small benefit-in-kind for assumed private use and a capped VAT deduction.

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Income-tax deduction: 100%** of the purchase as a professional cost. [astro]
- **Benefit in kind (Voordeel Alle Aard, VAA):** because private use is assumed, a fixed amount is added to the director's taxable income , for a tablet ~**EUR 36/year**, declared annually. [astro]
- **VAT: ~75% deductible** (the tax authority assumes partial private use). Claiming 100% is risky , a control can reject the full deduction and lead to corrections or penalties; the 75% rule is the safe, compliant choice. [astro]

> Confirm the current VAA amount and VAT percentage with [FOD Financiën](https://financien.belgium.be) before booking.

## Booking

Expense or capitalise per the amount and the firm's policy; book the non-deductible 25% VAT as part of the cost. Record the annual VAA for the director ([[payroll-director]], planned). The 100% income-tax deduction needs no add-back; only the VAA and the VAT cap apply.
