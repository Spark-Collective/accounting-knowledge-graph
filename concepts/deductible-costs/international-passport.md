# International passport

The cost of an international passport is deductible as a professional cost **when it is needed for genuine business travel** (a business trip, a client visit abroad, an international seminar).

## The rule

- **Work-related:** the travel the passport is for must have a business reason. [astro]
- **Proof of professional need:** keep evidence (an event invitation, a client-visit record) showing why the passport was required. [astro]
- **Pay via the professional account** and keep the payment proof (and the municipality's receipt for the application). [astro]

Like other small costs, this leans on the **professional-character test** ([[streaming-subscriptions]], [[reading-glasses]]): the burden is on the taxpayer to show the business need, so documentation matters more than the amount.

## Booking

Book to a class-6 travel/admin cost account (see [[chart-of-accounts-mar]]); attach the payment proof.
