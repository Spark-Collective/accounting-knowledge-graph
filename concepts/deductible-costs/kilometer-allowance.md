# Kilometer allowance (kilometervergoeding)

When the director uses their **own (private) car** for a professional trip, the company can pay a **forfait per kilometer** , tax-free and RSZ-free within the ceiling. This is the **private-car** case; a company car is handled via [[car-and-mobility]] + [[benefits-in-kind-vaa|VAA]] instead.

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Per-km rate (last-known):** ~**EUR 0.4326/km** (1 Jan-31 Mar 2026), ~**EUR 0.4327/km** (1 Apr-30 Jun 2026); the **annual** forfait (1 Jul 2025-30 Jun 2026) is ~**EUR 0.4449/km**. [search]
- **Tax- and RSZ-free** as long as the maximum is not exceeded. [search]
- For a director (bedrijfsleider), the amount must be reported on the **fiscal fiche 281.20**. [search]

> The rate is **indexed quarterly** (and a yearly variant exists). **Confirm the current per-km rate with [FOD Financiën](https://financien.belgium.be)** before reimbursing; over the ceiling becomes taxable.

## Why it matters

A simple, untaxed reimbursement for the common case of a director driving their private car for the BV , the agent applies the rate for the relevant quarter and logs the trips. Pairs with [[per-diem-domestic-travel]] (meals) for business travel.
