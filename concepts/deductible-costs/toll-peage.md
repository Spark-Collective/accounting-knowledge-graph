# Toll and peage costs

Toll / peage on a [[car-and-mobility|company car]] (e.g. driving abroad toward France or Italy) **can run through the company** as a professional cost , even on a holiday trip.

## The rule

- **Toll/peage is a company cost.** [astro]
- **Private use is already taxed via the car's [[benefits-in-kind-vaa|VAA]].** The company-car benefit covers not just the car itself but also fuel (home and abroad), car washes, and **toll/peage on holidays** , so a private holiday trip is captured through the VAA, not by disallowing the toll. [astro]

So the toll is booked as a company cost, and the private dimension is handled on the benefit-in-kind side rather than as a separate non-deductible item.

## Booking

Book to the car-cost group (class 6, see [[chart-of-accounts-mar]]); it follows the same CO2-based deductibility as the rest of the car costs in [[car-and-mobility]]. The private element flows through the VAA, not a separate add-back.
