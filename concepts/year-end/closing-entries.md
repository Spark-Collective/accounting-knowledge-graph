# Year-end closing entries

The set of entries posted at year-end, after the last [[month-end-close]], to bring the books to the position the [[annual-accounts-nbb|annual accounts]] report. Driven by the [[valuation-rules-advies|valuation rules]].

## The typical sequence

1. **Depreciation** , post the annual charge per the plan ([[depreciation]]).
2. **Impairments (waardeverminderingen)** , write down assets (debtors, inventory) below carrying value where needed.
3. **Provisions** , form/reassess/reverse provisions for risks and charges ([[provisions]]).
4. **Cut-off (overlopende rekeningen, 490-493)** , accrue costs/income spanning year-ends: prepaid charges, accrued income, accrued charges, deferred income.
5. **Inventory** , value stock and work in progress (acquisition value, or lower).
6. **Accrued interest / other accruals**.
7. **Corporate-tax provision** , book the estimated [[corporate-tax-basics|corporate tax]] for the year.
8. **Close the P&L** to the result, and the result to equity.

## Why it matters

These entries decide the year's result and the [[corporate-tax-basics|tax base]] (accounting profit + disallowed expenses). They are the substance of the [[year-end-close]] workflow.
