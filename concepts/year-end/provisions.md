# Provisions (voorzieningen) , practice

The working procedure for provisions for risks and charges, applying the principles in the [[provisions-advies|CBN advies]] and the [[valuation-rules-advies|valuation frame]].

## In the books

- The provision sits on the **liability side in rubric 16** (160-165) of the [[chart-of-accounts-mar|MAR]].
- **Forming/increasing** a provision is a cost: operating provisions via **635-637** (635 pensions, 636 large repairs/maintenance, 637 other operating risks), exceptional/non-recurrent via **662**.
- **Reversing** a non-recurrent provision is income via **762** (terugneming).
- The **year-to-year change** in the rubric-16 balance equals the algebraic sum of additions, uses, and reversals ([[provisions-advies]]).

## At year-end

- Account for **all foreseeable risks and probable losses** existing at the balance-sheet date, even if only known between the balance date and when the accounts are drawn up.
- **Reassess** each provision to a current estimate; do **not** keep an excess (no hidden reserves).
- A risk arising **after** the balance date is in principle not provided for in the current year (annuality).

## Why it matters

Provisions are a prudence-driven judgement that shapes the result and the [[corporate-tax-basics|tax base]] (some are disallowed). The agent forms/reverses them as part of [[closing-entries]] before the [[annual-accounts-nbb|annual accounts]].
