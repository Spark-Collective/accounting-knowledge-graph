# CBN advies , formation costs (oprichtingskosten)

A `references/cbn-adviezen/` page: the CBN/CNC treatment of **formation and expansion costs** , the setup costs of a company. Relevant when a new BV is incorporated.

## What the advies says

- Formation costs are **capitalized under rubric 20** (Oprichtingskosten) of the [[chart-of-accounts-mar|MAR]], unless taken to the result of the current year at once. They include incorporation, expansion, restructuring costs (notary fees, professional fees, loan-issue costs, ...). [cbn]
- **Amortized** on a suitable plan, **at least 20% per year** (so over a maximum of ~5 years), systematically and **independently of the year's result** ([[depreciation-advies|cf. depreciation]]). [cbn]
- **Restructuring costs** are only capitalized if they relate to a deep change with a **durable, favourable** effect on profitability. [cbn]
- **Loan-issue costs** may instead be spread over the loan term. [cbn]
- **Accelerated** amortization is allowed but disclosed; **additional** amortization is required if the real value falls below the net book value. [cbn]

## Grounds

[[chart-of-accounts-mar]] (rubric 20) and the early-life books of a new BV.
