# CBN advies , impairments (waardeverminderingen)

A `references/cbn-adviezen/` page: the CBN/CNC principles for **impairments** on receivables and inventory , a prudence-driven year-end write-down distinct from a [[provisions-advies|provision]]. Grounds [[closing-entries]].

## What the advies says

- Impairments follow the general principles (separate valuation, consistency, prudence, sincerity, good faith) and are **booked separately from the result** (CBN-advies 132/7). [cbn]
- **Receivables:** an impairment is booked **only when there is uncertainty** about realization or payment at maturity (for short- and long-term receivables alike). [cbn]
- **Inventory / work in progress:** write down to **net realizable value** when the production cost plus remaining costs exceeds the net selling price at year-end. [cbn]
- **Reversal:** reversing an impairment is **mandatory** when it has become excessive or pointless, booked under "terugneming van waardeverminderingen" in the P&L (an exception to the usual rule). [cbn]
