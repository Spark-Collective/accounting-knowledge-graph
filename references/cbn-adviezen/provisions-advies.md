# CBN advies , provisions for risks and charges (voorzieningen)

A `references/cbn-adviezen/` page: the CBN/CNC principles for **provisions for risks and charges**, a year-end judgement that grounds [[annual-accounts-nbb|the annual accounts]].

## What the advies says

- Provisions must meet the tests of **prudence, sincerity, and good faith**. [cbn]
- **Obligation to provide** (art. 33 KB W.Venn.): account for all foreseeable risks, possible losses, and impairments arising in the year (or prior years), even if only known **between the balance-sheet date and the date the accounts are drawn up**. [cbn]
- **Valuation:** the amount is adjusted at inventory date to a **current assessment** of the risk/charge. Provisions may **not be kept** to the extent they exceed what a current assessment requires (no hidden reserves). [cbn]
- A risk arising **after** the balance-sheet date is in principle not provided for in the current year (annuality/periodicity). [cbn]
- **Booking:** the year-to-year change in the liability item normally equals the algebraic sum of additions, uses, and reversals in the P&L. Exceptional provisions go through exceptional results unless the charge is operating/financial. [cbn]
