# CBN advies , research and development costs

A `references/cbn-adviezen/` page: the CBN/CNC treatment of **R&D costs**. Marginal for most one-person BVs, but relevant to a development-heavy company.

## What the advies says

- **Research costs: may NOT be capitalized** , since 1 January 2016 they are taken to the result immediately (KB of 18 December 2015; CBN-advies 138-1). [search]
- **Development costs: may be capitalized** under rubric **21 "Immateriële vaste activa"** (account 210), if **all** the conditions are met cumulatively: technical feasibility of completion is demonstrable; intention and ability to complete and use/sell the asset; probable future economic benefits; adequate technical/financial resources; and the expenditure can be measured reliably. [search]
- **Small companies may choose** to capitalize development costs or expense them directly , unless developing intangibles is their **main activity**. [search]
- **Amortization:** capitalized development costs are written off systematically over their useful life ([[depreciation-advies|cf. depreciation]]), with disclosure if accelerated.
